Welcome to episode one. We are Maya and Daniel – two people who ship small
software and wanted a show about the parts nobody writes conference talks
about.

## The advice gap

Most product advice assumes a growth team, a data warehouse, and a backlog of
A/B tests. That advice is not wrong – it is just for someone else. This show is
for the developer who is the design team, the support team, and the release
manager, all before lunch.

## What this show will not be

No funding-announcement victory laps, no ten-step frameworks. Just two people
talking honestly about the decisions that fill an ordinary week of building an
app. Glad you are here.
