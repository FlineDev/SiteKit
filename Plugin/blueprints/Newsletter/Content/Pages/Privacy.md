# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-01

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use this website and subscribe to our newsletter.

## 1. Data We Collect

### Newsletter Subscription
When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your **email address**. This is the only personal data we store.

### Website Analytics
This website does not use cookies or third-party tracking scripts. We may collect anonymous, aggregated server logs (IP addresses, user agents) for security and performance monitoring.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

Your email address is used exclusively to:
- Send you newsletter issues
- Send occasional service-related notifications (e.g., changes to this policy)

We will **never** sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

## 3. Email Delivery

Our newsletter is managed through a self-hosted email service. Emails are delivered via a third-party transactional email provider (e.g., Amazon SES). These providers process your email address solely for the purpose of delivering our messages and are bound by their own privacy policies.

## 4. Data Storage & Security

Your email address is stored on servers located in the European Union. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Rectify** inaccurate data
- **Erase** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restrict** processing of your data
- **Object** to processing of your data
- **Data portability** – receive your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

## 6. Unsubscribe

Every newsletter email contains an **unsubscribe link**. You can unsubscribe at any time with a single click. Upon unsubscription, your email address will be removed from our mailing list.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via the newsletter or on this page.
